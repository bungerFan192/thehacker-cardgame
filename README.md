## playing the game
1. "The night starts... All kids go to bed, tired...". Everyone lies down, head on table, hands, or anything on they can't see _ANYTHING_.
2. "And the hackers wake up." ONLY THE HACKERS can wake up. _Don't ruin the game, please_.
3. After the hackers discuss (obviously without talking), they _gently_, _without touching ANYONE ELSE_ touch someone. Hackers can also hack themselves, to make harder mysteries.
4. "The hackers, tired, also go to sleep.". The hackers 'go to sleep' by repeating step 1 for them.
5. "A new morning awaits, and someone got hacked.". The players have to quess who got hacked to 'save them'. If they don't in around 30 seconds, they lose the hacked person.
6. "The remaining kids now try to get the hacker.". The players have to guess a hacker. QRs do not help.
